About Us

We are the manufacturer of coconut shell briquettes in Bali. Our coconut charcoal briquettes manufactured from 100% natural, organically grown coconut shells without any mixes with wood or artificial chemicals.

  • Free from odor, sparks, and cracks.
  • We are manufacturing coconut charcoal briquettes and able to adjust quality and price according to the buyer specifications and requests.
  • We strictly control all stages from coconut shell selection, cleaning, carbonization to final briquette, drying, and packaging.

We provide a full package of export documents to bring your coconut charcoal briquetts to USA, Germany, Canada, Russia, Turkey, Australia. We can help you to arrange the shipment to your country with the best shipping lines.

Shipping and Export Details

Port Of Loading

Located at the Port of Benoa, Bali (DPS). We can help you to arrange the shipment to your country with the best shipping lines.

We do not serve LCL or partial loading, only full container load. Minimum Order is 18 MT / 20" Full Container Load.

Production Time

We strictly control all stages from coconut shell selection, cleaning, carbonization to final briquette, drying and packaging. We can produce up to 3.5 MT/Day.

Packaging

Export Documents

Payment Method
